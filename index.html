Our Program will empower managers to achieve the following

1

Build and manage data science teams suitable for their organizations

2

Evaluate different use cases for data science

3

Analyze the ethical issues and considerations for data science in their context

4

Explain the core concepts of data science including machine learning, Big Data, data storage, and data lifecycle

5

Develop plans for data science system implementation within their organizations and teams
